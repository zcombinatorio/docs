## Treasury Address

**Address:** `4Ckm4JKxJr6qZJHhoPnTkeVdV1qEPmt53hfVcFPCb5fU`

The treasury address holds all protocol assets and is **controlled entirely by futarchy governance**. All treasury operations are subject to governance approval through futarchy.

Treasury assets include:
- $ZC token holdings
- Protocol revenue and fees
- Strategic asset reserves

## Security Notes

<Warning>
  Always verify addresses before sending any transactions. Double-check addresses on official channels before interacting with the protocol.
</Warning>

- All protocol addresses are publicly verifiable on the Solana blockchain
- Treasury address is controlled by futarchy governance, not by any individual or team
- Changes to protocol addresses require governance approval through futarchy
